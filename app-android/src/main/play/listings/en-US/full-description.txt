This app adds Vancouver SkyTrain information to MonTransit.

This app provides the trains schedule (accessible offline) and news from buzzer.translink.ca as well as @TransLinkNews on Twitter.

These trains serve Vancouver, Burnaby, New Westminster, Richmond, Surrey in British Columbia, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display trains information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section below or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the data published by TransLink:
https://www.translink.ca/about-us/doing-business-with-translink/app-developer-resources

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-vancouver-translink-train-android

This app is not related with BCRTC (British Columbia Rapid Transit Company) or TransLink.

Permissions:
- Other: required to download news
