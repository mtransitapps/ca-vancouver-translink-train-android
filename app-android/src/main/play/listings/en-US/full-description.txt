This app adds Vancouver SkyTrains information to MonTransit.

This app provides the trains schedule and news from www.translink.ca and @TransLink and @TransLinkMedia on Twitter.

These trains serves Vancouver, Burnaby, New Westminster, Richmond, Surrey in British Columbia, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display trains information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by TransLink.
https://developer.translink.ca/ServicesGtfs/GtfsData

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-vancouver-translink-train-android

This app is not related with TransLink (South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority) and BCRTC (British Columbia Rapid Transit Company).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.translink.ca and Twitter
